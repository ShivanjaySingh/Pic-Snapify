How PhotoStudioPro Works

A simple, streamlined workflow that helps photo studios deliver photos professionally and lets clients select their favorites with ease.

📝

1. Studio Registers

Photo studio owners sign up, create their studio profile, and list their services on the platform.

💳

2. Choose a Plan

Studios select a subscription plan to unlock photo uploads, client galleries, and proofing features.

🖼️

3. Upload Galleries

Studios create private galleries and upload photos for each client or event.

❤️

4. Client Selection

Clients log in, view galleries, and select their favorite photos using online proofing.

📸 For Photo Studios

  • ✔ Create a professional studio profile
  • ✔ Manage multiple clients and events
  • ✔ Upload and organize photos easily
  • ✔ See client selections in real-time
  • ✔ Deliver photos without WhatsApp compression

👤 For Clients

  • ✔ Secure access to private galleries
  • ✔ View photos on any device
  • ✔ Like & shortlist favorite images
  • ✔ Simple, fast, and stress-free experience
  • ✔ Download final approved photos
