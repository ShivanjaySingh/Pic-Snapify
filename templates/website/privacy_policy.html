Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoStudio Pro collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective Date: 01 January 2025

PhotoStudio Pro ("we", "our", "us") operates the Studio Marketplace platform. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Studio and business profile details
  • Booking, subscription, and transaction records
  • Payment references processed by third-party gateways
  • Technical data including IP address and device information

2. Use of Information

  • To provide and maintain platform services
  • To process payments and subscriptions
  • To communicate service updates and notices
  • To ensure platform security and fraud prevention
  • To meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Payments

We do not store card or UPI information. All transactions are securely processed through certified payment service providers such as Razorpay.

4. Data Sharing

Your information is never sold. Data may be shared only with:

  • Payment processors for transaction completion
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law
  • Service providers assisting in platform operations

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Cookies

Cookies are used to maintain sessions and improve user experience. You may control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
